WELCOME

Frasers Cafe and Espresso Bar is a hugely popular cafe in Mount Eden Village which is always full to bursting with locals who come for a great coffee, warm service and one of their killer desserts. Frasers provides for more meals than your average hobbit, serving first breakfast right through to supper (they close at 11 pm)! Grab a prime position in the morning sun and watch the people of Mt Eden Village go about their merry (arty) way.
